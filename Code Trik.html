About Code Trik

Code Trik Nigeria Enterprises ; RC:2883467 is an electrical/software engineering firm. We expertice in:
  • Installation of smart automated electrical system for residential/cormercial structures.
  • Building of smart solar powered hybrid energy system; a clean and reliable green energy source as solution to interruption in grid supply.
  • Development of creative, reliable and incredible android mobile/desktop application as custom solution to bussiness and control challenges.

Mission Statement

Founded with the goal of solving Nigerians power challenges by combining clean green energy generation, control hardware, with mobile software to satisfy consumers need for 24/7 power availability and consumption management.
Extension of this reason is to provide installation services for a smart and automated electrical system, that can save clients from huge power bills by provides controls beyond conventional means.
Development of custom desktop, and Android applications solving your business or control challenges.

Our Drive

This company was born out of Nigerians need for stable and reliable electrical power and management of such power, as green energy (solar energy) is the present and future energy that individuals and commercial organizations can independently have, demand for smart means of managing this generated energy is increasingly rising.
Also born out of the need of Nigerians to have custom software (android mobile, desktop app) in managing there day in day out ethic business and control activities.

We train people

We offer Code tutoring services to train and breed newbies in software development to be proficient in Java and Kotlin programming languages for desktop and Android mobile application development.
